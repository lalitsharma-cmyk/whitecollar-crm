# Manager Training Guide — White Collar Realty CRM

This guide is for **Managers**. It shows you how to run your team's day, spot deals that are slipping, coach your agents with real numbers, and keep everyone moving — all from one screen.

You don't need any technical knowledge. If you can read a dashboard and have a coaching conversation, you have everything you need.

> **What a Manager sees:** You see **yourself and the agents who report to you** — their leads, calls, follow-ups, and pipeline. You can reassign leads, review the whole team's activity, and use every report for your team. (Only the Admin manages user accounts and roles.)

---

## 1. Start your day (the morning routine)

1. Open the CRM (**crm.whitecollarrealty.com** or the installed app).
2. On the **Dashboard**, tap **"I am here"** to punch in.
3. Glance at the **four hero tiles** at the top — they're your early-warning system:
   - **🔥 Hot leads untouched** — hot leads with no agent activity in 6+ hours.
   - **⏰ Overdue follow-ups** — promises your team hasn't kept.
   - **💎 Closable deals** — leads in Negotiation with an EOI stage set.
   - **🧊 Cold revival opportunities** — high-value leads dormant 30+ days.
   
   These tiles show numbers for your team. (Agents see only their own counts — which is intentional.)
4. Use the **team toggle** (Dubai / India / All) to focus on the team you're running today.

If any hero tile is high, that's where your morning attention goes.

---

## 2. Monitor your team

### The Dashboard
Below the hero tiles you'll find:
- **Sales Floor Live Feed** — the team's latest activity as it happens (calls logged, stages moved, follow-ups set). A quiet feed mid-morning means people need a nudge. This feed is also visible to agents — that's intentional; it keeps the team energised.
- **KPI tiles** — tiles show the time window and scope in their title (e.g. "Calls Dialed — today", "Total Clients — all time"). The "Cold→Lead" tile counts the whole calendar month.
- **By Salesperson table** — each agent's activity side by side. This is your fastest way to see who's busy and who's stalled. *(Managers and Admins only.)*
- **EOI / weighted forecast** — expected revenue, weighted by how likely each stage is to close.

### The Team & Roles page
Open **Team & Roles** (under the **Team** menu) for a deeper, per-agent scoreboard:
- **Active leads** and **Total calls** per agent
- **Workload** (green = healthy, amber = getting heavy, red = overloaded)
- **Pipeline value (90 days)**
- **Avg response time** (green = fast, red = slow)
- Each agent's **specializations** and **daily call target**

> **Coaching cue:** A red **Workload** chip means an agent is drowning — consider reassigning. A red **Avg response** means leads are waiting too long — coach on speed-to-lead.

---

## 3. Review follow-ups & today's actions

1. Open **Action List** — as a manager you can see the team's actions, grouped into:
   - **🔥 Ready to close**
   - **🚩 Need your attention**
   - **⏰ Follow-ups overdue**
2. The **Overdue** section is your priority. Overdue follow-ups are broken promises to clients — and the fastest way to lose a deal.
3. Open **Activities** (Action Board) to see the team's **⭐ Top 5 Actions**, hot follow-ups, and upcoming site visits.

> **Daily habit:** Aim to drive the team's **overdue** count toward zero by end of day.

---

## 4. Spot stuck & at-risk deals (the Pipeline)

Open **Pipeline** to see every deal as a board. The stages are:

**New → Contacted → Qualified → Site Visit → Negotiation → Booking Done**

The CRM colour-codes **momentum** so you can read trouble instantly:
- **Healthy** — moved within 3 days
- **Slowing** — 4–7 days without movement
- **Stuck** — more than 7 days stuck

Watch for these **risk chips** on cards:
- **"Stuck Nd"** — sitting in one stage for N days
- **"No activity since stage change"** — moved up but then ignored
- **"HOT lead going cold"** — a hot lead losing heat (act fast)
- **"Awaiting manager review"** — needs *you* to weigh in

> **Rule of thumb:** Any deal **stuck > 7 days** gets a coaching conversation today: *"What's the blocker? What's the next step? When's the next follow-up?"*

---

## 5. Coach your agents using CRM data

The CRM turns coaching from opinion into facts. Before a 1-on-1, pull these up:

1. **Avg response time** (Team & Roles) — is the agent slow to first contact? Coach speed.
2. **Pipeline momentum** — lots of "Stuck" cards? Coach on setting next steps and follow-up discipline.
3. **Remarks quality** — open a few of their leads and read the remarks. Shallow notes ("called, no answer") mean they're not capturing the client's real situation. Coach them to write the **full story** — budget, intent, objections, timeline.
4. **Follow-up discipline** — are leads missing a *next* follow-up date? That's the single most important habit to fix.
5. **Best-time-to-call heatmap** (Reports) — show the agent when *their* leads actually pick up, so they call at the right hours.

> **Coaching framing:** Lead with the data, then ask questions. *"I see five deals stuck over a week — walk me through them."* Let the agent reach the answer.

---

## 6. Reassign & balance the load

When an agent is overloaded, off, or a lead is being neglected:

1. Open the lead.
2. In the **Lead admin** section, use **Reassign** to move it to another agent.
3. Check the **Assignment history** to see how the lead has moved before (Managers/Admins can see this).

Use the **Workload** chips on Team & Roles to decide *who* should receive the lead — send it to someone in the green, not someone already in the red.

> **Awaiting Team:** Check the **Awaiting Team** page (red badge in the menu). Leads land here when their team (Dubai / India) hasn't been chosen yet — they will **not** auto-route until you pick a team. Tag them promptly so they start flowing to agents.

---

## 7. Handle escalations

When an agent taps **🆘 Escalate** on an action, it's flagged for you. Treat escalations as priority:
1. Open the flagged lead.
2. Read the conversation stream and the agent's remark.
3. Decide: coach the agent, jump on a call yourself, or reassign.
4. Always close the loop so the agent knows what happened.

---

## 8. Use reports for your team

Open **Reports** for the bigger picture. The **decisions strip** at the top gives you three answers instantly:
- **Forecasted revenue**
- **Biggest funnel leak** (the stage where most deals die)
- **Stalled deals > 7 days**

Useful reports for a manager:
- **Daily report** — what the team did today; use the **date picker** to look back at any day
- **SLA & Meetings** — are response times and meetings on track?
- **Cooling leads** — deals losing heat, ranked
- **Team comparison** — agents side by side
- **Lead sources** — where good leads come from *(source chart is Manager/Admin only)*
- **Pipeline overview** — health of the whole funnel

Every report has a **← Back to reports** link at the top, and a **shared date-range picker** so you can set any date window. The "Agent productivity" chart that used to appear has been removed — use the **By Salesperson table** on the Dashboard and the **Team & Roles** page instead. CSV export is Admin-only.

> Use **Team comparison** in team meetings — but to *lift everyone up*, not to shame. Pair every number with a next step.

---

## 9. Keep the team motivated

- **Leaderboards** — celebrate the top performers publicly; privately encourage the rest.
- **Missions & streaks** — remind the team these exist; a live streak keeps daily habits sticky.
- **☕ Daily note (pilot)** — if Lalit has switched this on for your team, a short motivational quote appears on everyone's dashboard each morning. It also has an optional **Listen** button the browser reads aloud. Encourage the team to tap it.
- **Team Mood / Vault** — keep a pulse on morale. A burnt-out team closes less. Notice it early.

---

## 10. What NOT to do

- ❌ **Don't** let **overdue follow-ups** sit. They're broken client promises.
- ❌ **Don't** ignore **"Awaiting Team"** leads — they don't route to anyone until you tag them.
- ❌ **Don't** pile new leads on an agent already showing a **red workload** chip.
- ❌ **Don't** coach on gut feel when the data is one click away — open the lead and read the remarks.
- ❌ **Don't** use the leaderboard to humiliate. Motivate.

# ⭐ Manager's One-Page Daily Checklist

**☀️ Morning**
- [ ] Punch in (**"I am here"**)
- [ ] Scan the **4 hero tiles** (Hot untouched / Overdue / Closable / Cold revival opportunities)
- [ ] Set today's focus team (Dubai / India / All) using the toggle
- [ ] Clear any **🆘 escalations**
- [ ] Tag any **Awaiting Team** leads so they start routing

**📊 Midday check-in**
- [ ] Watch the **Sales Floor Live Feed** — is everyone active?
- [ ] Review the **By Salesperson** table for stalled agents
- [ ] Work the **Pipeline** — flag every deal **stuck > 7 days**
- [ ] Rebalance **workload** (reassign from red → green agents)

**🎯 Coaching**
- [ ] Pick 1–2 agents; review **response time**, **momentum**, and **remark quality**
- [ ] Have one data-led coaching conversation
- [ ] Confirm every active lead has a **next follow-up date**

**🌙 End of day**
- [ ] Drive **overdue follow-ups** toward zero
- [ ] Glance at **Reports** decisions strip (forecast / leak / stalled)
- [ ] Recognise a win on the **Leaderboard**
- [ ] Note team **mood** — anyone need support tomorrow?
