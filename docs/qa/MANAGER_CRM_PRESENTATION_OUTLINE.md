## Slide 2 — Your scope in the CRM

**What you can and cannot see**

Can see:
- All leads assigned to agents on your team (Dubai or India)
- Your team's call logs and activity feed
- Reports for your team
- Leaderboard for your team

Cannot see:
- The other market team's data
- Admin tools (user management, settings toggles)

Key point: The "team" filter on every page refers to the market team you're assigned to — not the client's nationality or location.

## Slide 3 — Morning routine: Dashboard and Action List

**Start every day with these two pages**

Dashboard:
- Open /dashboard
- Look at the "By Salesperson" table: Calls, Overdue, Closeable, Needs [manager name]
- "Needs [your name]" = leads flagged for your personal intervention — these are your first calls of the day

Action List:
- Open /action-list
- Three sections: IN NEGOTIATION/EOI (close these today), NEED YOUR ATTENTION (flagged by agents), FOLLOW-UPS OVERDUE (slipping)

Demo:
- Live walkthrough of dashboard table
- Open /action-list and walk through a card

## Slide 4 — Pipeline review

**Spotting risk before deals fall apart**

Pipeline page (/pipeline):
- Use team filter to see your team only
- Momentum chips: green (healthy), amber (slowing), red (stuck)
- Red rings on cards = at-risk (hover to see reason)
- Sort order: highest-risk cards float to top

Moving a stage:
- Desktop: drag the card to the new column
- Mobile: tap "Move Stage" button on the card → bottom-sheet picker

Demo:
- Open /pipeline with team filter
- Show momentum chips and risk rings

## Slide 5 — Call Logs and Activity Feed

**Knowing what actually happened**

Call Logs (/call-logs):
- Filter by agent, outcome (Connected, Not Picked, etc.), date range
- Export as CSV for weekly reporting
- You only see your team's calls

Activity Feed (/reports/activity):
- Every call + every lead update for the selected date
- Use the date picker to review yesterday
- "(historical data)" label = MIS-imported entries, not from your current team

Demo:
- Open /call-logs, apply agent filter
- Open /reports/activity, use date picker

---

## Slide 6 — Leaderboard

**Running the weekly performance review**

Leaderboard (/reports/leaderboard):
- 90-day rolling window
- Sorted by calls made
- Shows: calls, leads assigned, qualified, won, conversion rate
- Gold/silver/bronze medals for top 3
- If the table shows all zeros: amber "no calls logged" banner appears
- Scoped to your team automatically

Use in weekly meetings:
- Recognise high performers
- Discuss anyone with high calls but low conversion (quality issue?)
- Discuss anyone with low calls (motivation or capacity issue?)

## Slide 8 — Key things to remember

**The rules of the road**

1. Your team assignment (Dubai or India) controls everything you can see — if the data looks wrong, check your team assignment with Lalit
2. Attendance is auto-marked when agents load the CRM — you do not need to chase them for check-ins
3. "Needs [your name]" on the dashboard is your most important column every morning
4. If a deal is stuck in NEGOTIATION for more than 7 days, the pipeline will show it as "stuck" — get on a call with the agent that day
5. You cannot add users or change settings — ask Lalit for those actions
