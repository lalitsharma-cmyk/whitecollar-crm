# Agent Training Guide — White Collar Realty CRM

Welcome! This is your simple, step-by-step guide to using the CRM every day.

You do **not** need to be technical. If you can use WhatsApp on your phone, you can use this. Read it once, keep it handy, and you'll be flying in a day or two.

> **What is this CRM?** It's our new home for every lead, every call, and every follow-up. No more lost numbers in WhatsApp, no more messy spreadsheets. Everything about your clients lives in one place — and it even tells you *who to call next*.

---

## 1. Log in for the first time

1. Open the link your manager sent you: **crm.whitecollarrealty.com**
2. Type your **email** and **password**.
3. Tap **Sign in**.

That's it. You're in.

> **Tip:** If you forget your password or can't get in, message Lalit. Don't keep trying — just ask.

---

## 2. Put the CRM on your phone (do this once)

You work from your phone, so let's make the CRM feel like a real app with its own icon.

**On Android (Chrome):**
1. Open **crm.whitecollarrealty.com** in Chrome.
2. Tap the **⋮** menu (top right).
3. Tap **Install app** (or **Add to Home screen**).
4. Confirm.

**On iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open the site in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the box with an arrow).
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add**.

Now you'll have a **White Collar** icon on your phone, just like any other app. Open it from there every day.

> **Tip:** When the CRM asks to send you **notifications**, tap **Allow**. That's how it reminds you about callbacks and follow-ups so you never forget one.

---

## 3. Your first 60 seconds every morning

Do these things the moment you start work:

1. **Open the app** (tap the icon on your phone).
2. At the very top of the **Dashboard** you'll see the **"I am here"** card — tap it to **punch in**. This tells the team you've started your day.
3. Just below, look for the **"Today's mission"** card — it highlights the single most important lead for you to act on right now (a deal to close, a hot lead to call, or an overdue follow-up).
4. Look for the **☕ Daily note** card if it appears — it's a short motivational quote to start the day. Tap **Listen** if you'd like it read aloud. *(This card is on a pilot — it may not be visible to everyone yet.)*

Then go straight to your **Action List**. That's where your day really begins.

> **Your numbers are private to you.** Every KPI tile on your dashboard — calls, follow-ups, ready-to-close deals — counts only YOUR leads. Your colleagues cannot see your numbers, and you cannot see theirs. The only shared screen is the **Sales Floor Live Feed** (the team activity ticker) and the Leaderboards — those exist so the whole team can celebrate wins together.

---

## 4. Use your Action List (this is your to-do list)

The **Action List** (also called **"To Do"** on the bottom menu) is the most important screen. It tells you exactly **who to contact and why** — you never have to guess.

It has three simple sections, in order:

- **🔥 Ready to close** — warm clients who are close to saying yes. Call these first.
- **🚩 Need your attention** — clients who need a nudge so they don't go cold.
- **⏰ Follow-ups overdue** — people you promised to call back. Don't make them wait.

Work from the **top down**. Each card shows the client's name, a short **"Next step"** hint, and quick buttons to **Call** or **WhatsApp** right away.

> **Golden rule:** Empty your Action List every day. If it's empty, you've done your job.

---

## 5. Call a lead

1. Open any lead (tap the client's name on your Action List or Leads page).
2. Tap the **📞 Call** button.
3. *(If calling is set up in your office)* Your **own phone will ring first** — answer it, and the CRM then dials the client for you automatically. Just talk normally.
4. When the call ends, a **"Log Call"** box pops up. **Always fill this in** (see next step).

> If the **Call** button asks you to set something up, tell Lalit — your calling may not be switched on yet. You can still phone the client normally and then log the call by hand (Step 7).

---

## 6. Send a WhatsApp

1. Open the lead.
2. Tap the **WhatsApp** button.
3. WhatsApp opens with the client's number ready. Type your message and send.

On a computer, you can also use the **WhatsApp panel** (the little WhatsApp icon at the top) to pop WhatsApp Web open beside the CRM, so you can chat and update notes side by side.

> **Tip:** After every WhatsApp chat, come back and add a quick **remark** so you remember what was said.

---

## 7. Log the call & add a remark (never skip this!)

Every time you talk to a client, tell the CRM what happened. It takes 10 seconds.

1. In the **Log Call** box, pick the **outcome**:
   - **Connected**, **Interested**, **Not interested**, **Callback**, **Not picked**, **Busy**, **Switched off**, or **Wrong number**.
2. In the **remark** box, write what the client said — in your own words. Example: *"Wants 2BHK in Dubai Marina, budget around 1.5M AED, will decide after talking to wife next week."*
3. Tap **Save**.

> **🎙 Too busy to type?** Tap the **microphone** icon and just **speak** your remark — in **Hindi or English**. The CRM types it for you.

**Why this matters:** Good remarks are how you (and your manager) remember every client's real story. Shallow notes like "called" help no one. Write the full situation.

---

## 8. Set a follow-up (the most important habit)

**Never end a conversation without setting the next follow-up date.** This is the #1 habit of top closers.

1. On the lead, find the **Scheduling** area (**Follow-up date**).
2. Pick the date/time you promised to contact them next.
3. Save.

If you chose **Callback** while logging a call, the CRM will ask you *when* — pick the time, and it will **remind you automatically** before the callback.

> **Golden rule, again:** Every client always has a *next* date. No client should ever be "forgotten."

---

## 9. Move a lead along the pipeline

As a client gets more serious, move them up the ladder so everyone can see progress.

The stages are:

**New → Contacted → Qualified → Site Visit → Negotiation → Booking Done**

1. Open the lead (or open the **Pipeline** screen).
2. Change the **stage** to the right step.
3. Save.

**On the lead page you'll also see:**

- **WHO IS THE CLIENT** — a dropdown. Pick **Investor**, **End-user**, **Both**, or **Unclear**. This is now a click-to-select, not a text box. Below it is a free-text "Client context" notes box for the full story.
- **BANT** card (Budget · Authority · Need · Timeline) — four colour-coded chips showing how well you've qualified the client. A **"N/4 captured"** pill at the top tells you at a glance how complete the qualification is. Fill these in as you learn more about the client.
- **"Why this score"** — a breakdown explaining the client's HOT / WARM / COLD score. *(Fully live once AI is switched on by Lalit; rule-based explanation always visible.)*

Simple rule of thumb:
- Talked to them once? → **Contacted**
- They have real budget + intent? → **Qualified**
- Booked a property viewing? → **Site Visit**
- Discussing price/payment? → **Negotiation**
- They said yes! → **Booking Done** 🎉

---

## 9b. Adding a new lead

When you fill in the **New Lead** form and enter a phone number, the CRM will automatically show a yellow **"possible duplicate"** warning if that number already exists in the system. This is a non-blocking warning — you can still save the lead. It's there to stop the same client being entered twice by accident.

---

## 10. Work your cold / revival leads

Some leads go quiet. They're not dead — they just need reviving. That's what the **Revival Engine** (the **💎** screen, also called Cold Calls) is for.

1. Open **Revival Engine**.
2. Tap **🎯 Start session (20 leads)**.
3. Work through them one by one — call, WhatsApp, log, set a follow-up.
4. If a cold lead gets interested again, tap **Promote to Lead** to bring them back into your active pipeline.

There's a **Daily Revival Mission** and a streak counter here — keep your streak alive and climb the leaderboard!

---

## 11. Complete your tasks & activities

The **Action Board** (Activities) shows your **⭐ Today's Top 5 Actions** plus hot follow-ups, slipping leads, and any site visits coming up.

- When you finish something, tap **✅ Complete**. You earn **XP** for it!
- Can't do it right now? Tap **⏸ Snooze**.
- Stuck and need help? Tap **🆘 Escalate** to flag it for your manager.

---

## 12. Track your progress (have fun with it!)

- **Leaderboards** — see how you rank against the team. A little friendly competition goes a long way.
- **Missions & XP** — finish actions, hit targets, earn points, keep your streak.
- **Vault** — your private space to journal wins, vent, or hit **Reset Mode** on a tough day. It's just for you.

> Sales is a marathon. Celebrate small wins, keep your streak, and don't be hard on yourself on slow days.

---

## 13. What NOT to do

- ❌ **Don't** end a call or chat without setting the **next follow-up date**.
- ❌ **Don't** leave the **remark** blank or write just "called." Write the real story.
- ❌ **Don't** let your **Action List** pile up. Clear it daily.
- ❌ **Don't** forget to **punch in** ("I am here") each morning.
- ❌ **Don't** share your login with anyone.
- ❌ **Don't** worry about other people's leads — you'll only ever see **your own**. Focus on yours. The Sales Floor feed and Leaderboard show team activity — that's intentional and fine.
- ❌ **Don't** look for a "Copy Snapshot" or "Export CSV" button on a lead — those were removed. If you need a lead printout, ask Lalit.
- ❌ **Don't** panic if something looks confusing. Ask Lalit. There's no "delete everything" button you can press by accident — your data is safe.
